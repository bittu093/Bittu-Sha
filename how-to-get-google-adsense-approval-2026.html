How to Get Google AdSense Approval in 2026 (Step‑by‑Step Guide)

jan 10, 2026 8 min read 2,450 views

Getting Google AdSense approval in 2026 is not difficult if you do the right things. Many beginners get rejected because of small mistakes — not because their website is bad. In this guide, I’ll explain exact steps to get AdSense approval fast, even if your website is new.

What Is Google AdSense?

Google AdSense is a program that lets you earn money by showing ads on your website. When visitors see or click ads, you earn money. AdSense is one of the easiest ways to monetize a website, especially for beginners.

It works best for:

  • Blogs
  • Educational websites
  • Personal websites
  • Small business websites
  • Niche content sites

Minimum Requirements for AdSense Approval (2026)

  • ✅ Original content (not copied)
  • ✅ Easy navigation
  • ✅ Important pages
  • ✅ Clean design
  • ✅ Domain age (even new domains can be approved)

    • Step 1: Choose the Right Website Type

    Google prefers websites that help users.

    Best website types for approval:

  • How‑to blogs
  • Educational content
  • Personal experience blogs
  • Tech / career / finance blogs

    • ❌ Avoid:

  • Download-only sites
  • Copyrighted content
  • Thin AI‑generated content

    • Step 2: Write High‑Quality Content (Most Important)

    Content quality decides approval.

    How many posts are required?

  • Minimum: 5–7 good posts
  • Recommended: 8–12 posts

    • Content rules:

  • Each post: 800–1200 words
  • Simple English
  • Clear headings (H1, H2)
  • Helpful, original information

    • Content rules:

  • Best topics:
  • How to earn money online
  • SEO & blogging tips
  • Student guides
  • Technology tutorials

    • Step 3: Add Mandatory Pages (Must Have)

    These pages are mandatory:

    1. Privacy Policy
    2. Terms of Service
    3. Contact Page
    4. About Us Page

    Make sure:

    1. Pages are visible in footer or menu
    2. Contact page has a working form or email

    Step 4: Use a Clean & Professional Design

    Your website should look trustworthy.

    Design checklist:

    • Simple layout
    • Readable fonts
    • Fast loading speed
    • Mobile-friendly
    • No broken links

    Free themes are okay — design quality matters, not price.

    Step 5: Create & Submit ads.txt (After Applying)

    After applying for AdSense:

  • Google gives an ads.txt code
  • Upload it to your root domain

    • Example:

    google.com, pub-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX, DIRECT, f08c47fec0942fa0

    This helps Google verify ownership.

    Step 6: Apply for Google AdSense Correctly

    Steps:

    1. Go to Google AdSense
    2. Sign in with your Google account
    3. Click "Get Started"
    4. Fill in your website details
    5. Review and accept the terms
    6. Submit your application

    Step 7: Wait for Review (Be Patient)

    AdSense review time in 2026:

  • Usually: 3–7 days
  • Sometimes: up to 14 days

    • Status you may see:

  • Getting ready
  • Ready
  • Needs attention
  • All are normal.

    • Common Reasons for AdSense Rejection

    Avoid these mistakes:

  • Copied content
  • Very few posts
  • Empty pages
  • Broken links
  • Adult or misleading content
  • Fix issues and reapply after 2–3 weeks.

    • Pro Tips to Get Approved Faster

  • Add 1–2 new posts after applying
  • Share website link on social media
  • Don’t change theme during review
  • Avoid excessive ads or scripts

    FAQ: Google AdSense Approval Guide (2026)

    1. What is Google AdSense and how does it work?

    Google AdSense is an advertising program that allows website owners to earn money by showing ads. You earn money when visitors view or click ads displayed on your website.

    2. How can I get Google AdSense approval?

    To get AdSense approval, your website must have original content, a clean design, and important pages like Privacy Policy, About Us, and Contact page. Follow Google policies and apply correctly.

    3. How many blog posts are required for AdSense approval?

    There is no fixed number, but it is recommended to have at least 5–10 high-quality blog posts before applying for Google AdSense.

    4. Does Google AdSense require traffic?

    No, Google AdSense does not require minimum traffic. Even a new website can get approved if the content is helpful and policy-compliant.

    5. How long does Google AdSense approval take?

    AdSense approval usually takes 3–7 days. In some cases, it may take up to 14 days.

    6. Why does Google AdSense reject websites?

    Common reasons include thin content, copied content, missing pages, policy violations, or poor website structure. Fix the issues and reapply after a few weeks.

    7. Can I apply for AdSense without investment?

    Yes. You can create a website using free tools and apply for AdSense without any investment. The main requirement is quality content.
