Learn how to earn money from Facebook Reels, posts, stories, and ads in India in 2026

Facebook monetization has opened new doors for creators and small business owners to earn online. From Reels to sponsored content, Facebook offers multiple revenue streams. In this complete guide, we’ll walk you through all the methods to monetize your Facebook presence effectively.

1. Monetize Facebook Reels

Facebook Reels monetization allows creators to earn money directly from short videos. You can earn through ad breaks placed in your Reels once your page meets eligibility requirements.

  • Upload engaging, high-quality short videos consistently.
  • Focus on niches like tech, finance, education, or entertainment.
  • Ensure your page has at least 10,000 followers and 30,000 one-minute views in the last 60 days.

2. Facebook In-Stream Ads

In-stream ads let you earn money from ad placements in your videos. Ads are displayed either before, during, or after your content.

  • Eligible pages need 10,000 followers and 600,000 minutes of viewed content over 60 days.
  • Maintain original content to comply with Facebook’s monetization policies.
  • Higher engagement increases revenue from ads.

3. Fan Subscriptions

Fan subscriptions allow followers to support you with a monthly payment. In return, you can offer exclusive content, badges, and perks.

  • Offer special content only available to subscribers.
  • Promote subscriptions through posts, Reels, and live streams.
  • Even small pages can start with 100–500 subscribers, earning ₹5,000–₹20,000 monthly.

4. Facebook Stars

Facebook Stars let fans send virtual gifts during live streams, which can be converted into real money.

  • Encourage engagement during live sessions to increase Stars.
  • Interactive and entertaining content works best.
  • Each Star is worth $0.01, which can accumulate quickly with an active audience.

5. Sponsored Content & Brand Deals

Brands pay Facebook creators for promotions and sponsored posts. Your earning depends on your niche, audience size, and engagement rate.

  • Reach out to brands related to your content niche.
  • Use platforms like influencer marketing networks to get collaborations.
  • Even small pages can earn ₹5,000–₹50,000 per sponsored post.

FAQ: Facebook Monetization Complete Guide

1. What is Facebook monetization?

Facebook monetization allows content creators, page owners, and video publishers to earn money through ads, fan subscriptions, branded content, and in-stream video ads.

2. Who can monetize on Facebook?

To monetize on Facebook, you need to meet eligibility requirements such as having at least 10,000 followers or 600,000 total minutes of viewed content in the last 60 days for pages. Individual creators can also monetize through Facebook Reels or fan subscriptions.

3. How much can I earn from Facebook monetization?

Earnings depend on your content type, audience size, engagement rate, and ad revenue. Many small creators earn ₹10,000–₹50,000 monthly, while larger pages and influencers can earn ₹1,00,000+ monthly.

4. How do I enable monetization on my Facebook page?

Go to Facebook Creator Studio → Monetization, check eligibility, and follow the steps to connect a payment account. You must follow Facebook’s policies and content guidelines to activate monetization.

5. What are common monetization methods on Facebook?

Common methods include in-stream video ads, fan subscriptions, branded content partnerships, and Facebook Stars (for live streams). Choosing the right method depends on your audience and content type.
